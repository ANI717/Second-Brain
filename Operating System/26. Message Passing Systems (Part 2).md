
##### Direct Communication (Type 1)
- send(P, message)
- receive(Q, message)
- Between a pair of processes exists only one link
- Symmetry in addressing

##### Direct Communication (Type 2)
- send(P, message)
- receive(id, message)
- Asymmetry in addressing

##### Indirect Communication
- send or receive by mailbox or port
- send(A, message)
- receive(A, message)
- Link can be associated among more than two processes
- Mailbox can be owned my process or OS
